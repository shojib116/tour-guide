Visit Savar

Enjoy culture and diverse lifestyle just outside the capital Dhaka.

Top three activities to do at Savar

Visit the National Martyr's Memorial

The National Martyr's Memorial is a great place to get a glimpse at the history of Bangladesh!

Go for a picnic in the local parks

Fantasy Kingdom is perfect for having fun with family and friends with versatile rides and two other parks for recreation.

Enjoy the scenic beauty of Jahangirnagar

The Jahangirnagar University campus is the goto place if you want a fresh breath and enjoy the scenic beauty
